Captain Janeway, we are in dire need of your expert assistance! The aliens have attacked and we need you to defeat them!

Next

Your mission is to get the aliens' health to 0 before they can do the same to yours. You both will begin at 100 health and each have the option to attack, heal, or defend.

Next

If you attack, you will use one of your three weapons: gun (5 damage), cannon (10 damage), and bomb (40 damage). Unfortunately our earth systems are on the fritz and which weapon you are able to use is up to chance!

If you heal, you will regenerate your shields (and health) by 10 health points.

If you defend, your shields will go up and any attack by the aliens their next turn will be absorbed (no damage will be done).

Remember though: alien technology is similar to earth's --- your alien enemies will be making decisions from the same options as earth!
